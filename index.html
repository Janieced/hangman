SneakerHead Hangman!

1. Start the game by pressing any key.
2. Each word will be the name of a popular sneaker.
3. Press the letter on the keyboard that you would like to guess.
4. If your selected letter is in the word it will display on the screen. If not it will display in the "Letters already Guessed" column.
5. If you guess the word correctly before you run out of tries your score will increase by one in the "Wins" column.
6. If you run out of guess without guessing the correct word your score will increase by one in the "Losses" column.
Good Luck!
